Product Features

New and Outstanding Features

Everybody loves getting more bang for their buck, which may explian why most digital cameras offer lots of functions.

Removable Memory Card

All but the most inexpensive digital cameras save images to memory cards. The capacity of memory cards varies widely.

Video

Most cameras offer low resolution video capability that's appropriate for web. Better cameras feature higher frame rates and full-frame size.

Voice Recording

Use your camera to save conversations about your pictures to capture sound around you.

Digital Zoom

Digital zoom merely spreads the pixels apart to appear to extend your telephoto Lens while causing significant image degradation.
